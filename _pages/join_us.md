We’re always on the lookout for people who are enthusiastic, motivated, and love to tinker. Many of the things we do live at the intersection of different expertise -  molecular biology, microscopy, data analysis, ecology, electronics, and computer science. If our research areas excite you, or you enjoy working across disciplines to build, test, and explore, we’d love to hear from you!

**Graduate Students (PhD Program)**

- We accept graduate students through the TIGS PhD program. Details and announcements can be found [here](https://tigs.res.in/impact/phd-program/).

**MSc Thesis Students**

- Each year, we host one or two MSc thesis students from different universities. If you’re interested, get in touch!

**How to Join**

- Send an email to sonia.sen@tigs.res.in with your CV and a short note about your background and what interests you.
